About

A walk into the doors of Codifica is an easy indicator of what we’re about. It’s simple. We love coding, and we love coffee. We believe the best way to combine our interests together is by turning caffeine into beautiful software. Codifica aims to provide the ideal hackerspace for anyone with an artistic mind, creating a close-knit community in the process.


We are huge advocates of teaching passionate people how to code.”
